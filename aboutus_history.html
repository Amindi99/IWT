Our History

TCS originally started in early 2014. Originally based in Sri Lanka, It grew to a team of over 50 Linux Support Engineers before our founder, Prashan De Seram established TCS to serve an emerging market. There was an underserved customer support needed for fast-growing tech startups. After much research, Prashan established an office in the Sri Lanka and grew the company to a team of over 100 members before appointing fellow entrepreneur, Mihiran Rupasinghe to be CEO.
